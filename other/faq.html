Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Dota 1x6?

Dota 1x6 is a custom game mode in Dota 2 where 6 players fight in a free-for-all arena using perks, builds and survival strategies.

❓ How do I choose the right perks?

Pick perks that synergize with your hero's strengths. If you're a magic user, prioritize cooldowns and spell damage; for right-clickers, go for damage or lifesteal.

❓ Why do I die so fast?

You may be building incorrectly or overextending. Learn item timing and avoid fighting stronger enemies early.

❓ How do I choose the right hero for my playstyle?

Choosing the right hero depends on your preferred role, mechanics comfort, and game sense.

Here are some tips to help:

  • Aggressive/Frontline playstyle → Try Strength heroes like Centaur, Alchemist, or Axe.
  • Farming and fast-paced scaling → Agility heroes such as Anti-Mage or Juggernaut may suit you.
  • Tactical or spell-based players → Intelligence heroes like Lina or Zeus offer lots of skill-based potential.
  • Press one button and win → Try heroes like Wraith king, Bristleback, drowranger.
